ABOUT US


Odyssey 2017 - The cultural festival of HBTU-K is being organized during the month of February (25th-27th). It is one of the largest Cultural festivals of India with participation of over 2500 students, in addition to the 10,000 strong student community and 500 non-campus residents from 50 colleges around the country offering an unparalleled competitive environment where they showcase their talent and compete with the best upcoming technocrats in the country in a highly charged and competitive ambience.

The odyssey 2k17 invites you all to show up your skills to refine your extra talents.
